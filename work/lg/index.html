LG Mobile

Account and Community Management + Strategy

Agency: Ogilvy

LG Mobile launched the G5 cell phone and needed to find their way in a crowded iPhone and Samsung world.

My task was to assist with the creative strategy, provide cultural insights and heavily monitor the social channels while doing community management. This included collaborating on features in media outlets, showcasing its eye-catching abilities and accessories.

Through countless hours of work, my team developed the award-winning "Low Battery Anxiety" campaign featured in outlets such as Cosmopolitan, The Guardian, and Men's Health.

We aimed to achieve an affinity for LG among millenials with strategically curated lifestyle engagements and events, integrated brand stories, issues management, product placements, and product test drive.
