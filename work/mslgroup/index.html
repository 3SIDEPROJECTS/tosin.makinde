Emirates Airline

Account, Strategy, and Community Management

Agency: MSLGROUP

When it comes to luxury and air travel, Emirates is usually one of the first that you think of. With that in mind, my job was to prepare for the Emirates Airline launch of their new route from Newark, New Jersey, to Athens, Greece.

On the Emirates Airline account, I developed briefs and media kits while focusing on the audience to create influencer campaigns. Using Emirates' luxurious amenities, we announced the route launch in style.
